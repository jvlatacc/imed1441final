Upgrade Account Status
Another tweak that may benefit users
who have placed orders prior as a guest
would involve the ability to convert
from a guest user to a full user during the
same session. Currently if you add
items to a cart while browsing as a guest
and you then hit Not Guest Shopper
it clears the cart and returns you to a
new session.
If a user has customized items and
placed them in their cart they may
 not go back through the steps again
when the cart is cleared. The Not
Guest Shopper link should be replaced
with a “Add this order to an existing
account” link or something similar.
